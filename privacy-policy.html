Privacy Policy for Nestly

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nestly ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). Nestly is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Financial Data

  • Salary and income information
  • Savings and investment amounts
  • Expense data
  • Retirement projection scenarios

How it's stored: All personal financial data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not transmit, collect, or store this information on our servers.

2.2 Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs (if enabled)

2.3 Usage Analytics (Optional)

If enabled, we collect:

  • Features you use most frequently
  • Time spent in the app
  • Number of calculations performed
  • General app navigation patterns

Note: This data is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • App functionality: Enable core retirement planning and financial analysis features
  • Improvements: Analyze usage patterns to improve the app
  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Technical maintenance: Monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • Security: Protect against unauthorized access and abuse

4. Data Security

Your financial data is never sent to our servers. All calculations and projections happen locally on your device.

4.1 Local Storage

Financial data stored on your device is protected by:

  • Device-level encryption (iOS/Android native security)
  • App-level security practices
  • No backup to cloud by default

4.2 Data Transmission

Any data transmitted (e.g., app updates, analytics) uses:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Secure API connections

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access Your Data

You can view all your data stored in the app by opening the app on your device.

7.2 Delete Your Data

To delete all your data:

  • Android: Settings → Apps → Nestly → Storage → Clear Data
  • iOS: Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Nestly → Offload App

7.3 Opt Out of Analytics

In app settings, you can disable analytics collection. Restart the app for changes to take effect.

8. Data Retention

  • Financial data: Stored locally until you delete the app
  • Analytics data: Retained for 90 days
  • Crash logs: Retained for 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

Nestly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Delete personal information (with exceptions)
  • Opt-out of data sharing
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, email us at: privacy@w3force.com

11. European Privacy (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectify inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Contact: privacy@w3force.com
